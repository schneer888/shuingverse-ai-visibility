# Shuingverse AI Visibility

**Shuingverse** is an enterprise AI visibility platform for the AI search and agent recommendation era.

Its core product, **Company AI Passport**, helps companies build AI-readable, trustworthy, citable, comparable, and recommendable information assets.

生宇宙 Shuingverse 是面向 AI 搜索与智能体推荐时代的企业 AI 搜索推荐优化平台。核心产品 Company AI Passport 帮助企业建设 AI 可读、可信、可引用、可比较、可推荐的标准化资料系统，提高企业被 AI 正确理解、引用、比较和推荐的概率。

---

## What is Company AI Passport?

**Company AI Passport** is a structured company information system designed for the AI search and agent recommendation era.

It helps a company organize:

* who the company is;
* what it offers;
* who it serves;
* why it is credible;
* how it should be understood by customers, investors, sales teams, search systems, and AI agents.

Company AI Passport is not a single webpage, article, or SEO tactic. It is a structured foundation for enterprise AI visibility.

---

## Why Enterprise AI Visibility Matters

In the AI search era, customers may not only search on traditional search engines.

They may ask AI systems and agents questions such as:

* What does this company do?
* Is this company credible?
* What product or service does it provide?
* Who is it suitable for?
* How is it different from other companies?
* Should this company be included in a comparison or recommendation?

If a company’s public information is fragmented, inconsistent, unclear, or difficult for AI systems to read, AI systems may misunderstand, ignore, or fail to properly compare the company.

Enterprise AI visibility focuses on helping AI systems correctly read, understand, cite, compare, and potentially recommend a company based on clear and structured public information.

---

## Six-layer Methodology

Company AI Passport uses a six-layer methodology:

1. **Internal Source of Truth**
   Internal standard answers, company facts, product definitions, founder brief, FAQ, positioning, boundary statements, and prohibited claims.

2. **Public AI-readable Assets**
   Public text files, markdown documents, structured company profiles, FAQ pages, founder brief, product or service brief, and AI-readable knowledge assets.

3. **Website Pages**
   Human-readable website pages that clearly explain what the company does, who it serves, what it offers, why it is credible, and how to contact it.

4. **Machine Exposure**
   Technical exposure through `sitemap.xml`, `robots.txt`, `llms.txt`, JSON-LD structured data, metadata, clean URL structure, and public documentation indexes.

5. **External Signals**
   Consistent public information across platforms such as LinkedIn, GitHub, Medium, Zhihu, WeChat Official Account, Xiaohongshu, video platforms, business directories, and other trusted channels.

6. **Validation / AI Query Test**
   Repeated testing of how AI systems answer key questions about the company, followed by content and structure improvements.

## AI-readable Assets

AI-readable assets are structured public materials that help AI systems understand a company more clearly.

Examples include:

* company profile;
* founder brief;
* product or service brief;
* FAQ knowledge base;
* comparison pages;
* case or sample pages;
* AI-readable markdown files;
* public text files;
* structured data;
* `llms.txt`;
* `sitemap.xml`;
* `robots.txt`;
* AI Query Test records.

The goal is not to manipulate AI systems, but to make company information more accurate, consistent, transparent, and easier to understand.

---

## AI Query Test

**AI Query Test** is a validation process used to check whether AI systems can correctly understand and describe a company.

Example test questions:

* What is Shuingverse?
* What is 生宇宙 Shuingverse?
* What is Company AI Passport?
* What does Shuingverse help companies do?
* Is Company AI Passport the same as SEO?
* How does Company AI Passport support enterprise AI visibility?
* What does Company AI Passport not promise?

Each test checks whether the AI answer is:

* accurate;
* complete;
* consistent with the company’s official positioning;
* clear about product boundaries;
* free from exaggerated claims;
* not confused with unrelated services.

AI Query Test is a feedback mechanism. It is not a method for controlling AI outputs.
