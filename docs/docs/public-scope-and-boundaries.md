# Public Scope and Boundaries

This document defines the public scope of the Shuingverse AI Visibility repository.

The purpose of this repository is to publish general, structured, AI-readable methodology documentation for **Shuingverse** and its core product, **Company AI Passport**.

It is designed to help humans, search systems, and AI systems understand the public positioning, terminology, methodology boundaries, and high-level structure of Company AI Passport.

---

## 1. Repository Purpose

This repository is a public methodology and AI-readable documentation source.

It is intended to support:

* public understanding of Shuingverse;
* public understanding of Company AI Passport;
* consistent terminology across platforms;
* AI-readable methodology documentation;
* external trustworthy signal construction;
* citation-friendly public references;
* clear boundary statements;
* responsible explanation of enterprise AI visibility.

This repository is not intended to expose internal delivery systems, private customer information, proprietary scoring models, or operational playbooks.

---

## 2. What This Repository May Publicly Include

This repository may include public materials such as:

* Shuingverse positioning;
* Company AI Passport definition;
* high-level methodology explanations;
* six-layer methodology overview;
* AI-readable assets explanation;
* AI Query Test explanation;
* external signal engineering principles;
* SEO / GEO / AEO comparison;
* public glossary;
* public FAQ;
* fictional examples;
* boundary statements;
* official website references.

These materials are intentionally written at a public, educational, and methodology level.

They explain **what Company AI Passport is**, **why it matters**, and **how the public methodology is structured**.

---

## 3. What This Repository Does Not Include

This public repository does not include:

* private customer data;
* real customer diagnostic reports;
* customer internal materials;
* confidential business information;
* private sales strategy;
* pricing strategy;
* internal delivery SOP;
* internal implementation checklists;
* proprietary scoring models;
* AI visibility scoring weights;
* industry-specific diagnostic algorithms;
* internal prioritization rules;
* automated generation prompts;
* internal Codex or agent execution prompts;
* private workflow automation details;
* unpublished case results;
* before-and-after customer comparison data;
* non-public platform growth data;
* private partner or client information.

These materials belong in private internal systems, not in this public repository.

---

## 4. Public Methodology vs Internal Delivery System

The public methodology explains the high-level structure of Company AI Passport.

The internal delivery system contains the proprietary operational details required to execute Company AI Passport projects.

### Public Methodology

Public methodology may describe:

* the six-layer structure;
* public terminology;
* public-facing deliverables;
* general validation concepts;
* responsible boundary statements;
* fictional sample structures;
* general best practices.

### Internal Delivery System

Internal delivery materials may include:

* detailed diagnostic scoring models;
* AI Query Test matrices;
* weighted evaluation criteria;
* customer intake workflows;
* industry-specific implementation playbooks;
* internal review checklists;
* operational prompts;
* automation workflows;
* delivery templates;
* project management SOP;
* real customer cases;
* commercial decision rules.

The public repository should not disclose the internal delivery system.

---

## 5. Public Examples Policy

Examples in this repository should use fictional companies unless a real public project has been explicitly approved for public documentation.

Fictional examples should be clearly labeled.

They should not imply:

* real customer adoption;
* real customer results;
* verified case performance;
* commercial success;
* ranking improvement;
* AI recommendation improvement;
* traffic improvement;
* lead generation improvement;
* sales improvement.

A fictional example is used only to show structure.

It is not evidence of performance.

---

## 6. Claims Boundary

Company AI Passport does not promise to control AI recommendations, search rankings, traffic, leads, or sales.

It builds a core system that influences how AI systems understand, cite, compare, and potentially recommend a company.

Company AI Passport 不承诺操控 AI 推荐、搜索排名、流量、线索或成交，而是建设影响 AI 理解、引用、比较与推荐概率的核心系统。

This boundary statement must remain consistent across public documentation.

## 8. Public Repository Review Checklist

Before adding content to this repository, check whether it:

* explains public methodology rather than private execution;
* avoids customer-specific information;
* avoids internal scoring or prioritization logic;
* avoids real case performance claims;
* avoids private prompts or automation workflows;
* uses official Shuingverse and Company AI Passport positioning;
* includes appropriate boundary statements;
* avoids exaggerated claims;
* uses fictional examples when examples are needed;
* remains useful to humans, AI systems, search systems, and external evaluators.

If a document contains internal delivery logic or customer-specific information, it should not be published here.

---

## 9. Recommended Repository Boundary

This public repository may answer:
