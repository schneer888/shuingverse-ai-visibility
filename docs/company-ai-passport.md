# Company AI Passport

Company AI Passport is the core product of Shuingverse, an enterprise AI visibility platform for the AI search and agent recommendation era.

It helps companies build AI-readable, trustworthy, citable, comparable, and recommendable information assets.

Official website: https://www.shuingverse.com/

## Problem

AI search engines and agent systems increasingly answer questions by synthesizing information from public sources. Many companies still present their information in ways that are fragmented, inconsistent, hard to cite, or difficult for machines to compare.

Common problems include:

- Important company facts are scattered across websites, PDFs, social profiles, and third-party pages.
- Product and service descriptions use inconsistent terminology.
- Public pages are designed mainly for human visitors, with limited machine-readable structure.
- AI systems may lack reliable sources for company identity, offerings, categories, geography, target customers, and proof points.
- Teams do not regularly test how AI systems understand or describe the company.

## Definition

Company AI Passport is a standardized company information system designed for AI search and agent recommendation environments.

It organizes verified company information into public, structured, citation-friendly assets that help AI systems understand:

- Who the company is
- What the company offers
- Which categories and scenarios it belongs to
- How it compares with relevant alternatives
- Which sources can be cited
- Which external signals support the company's public identity

## Positioning

Shuingverse is an enterprise AI visibility platform for the AI search and agent recommendation era. Its core product, Company AI Passport, helps companies build AI-readable, trustworthy, citable, comparable, and recommendable information assets.

Company AI Passport is not a shortcut for search ranking or AI recommendation. It is a methodology and infrastructure layer for improving the quality, consistency, and accessibility of company information.

## Target Users

Company AI Passport is designed for organizations that need public company information to be accurately understood and reused by humans and AI systems.

Typical users include:

- B2B companies
- SaaS companies
- Manufacturers and suppliers
- Professional service firms
- Technology companies
- Export-oriented companies
- Marketing, brand, communications, and growth teams
- Founders and leadership teams responsible for company positioning accuracy

## Six-Layer Construction Methodology

Company AI Passport uses a six-layer methodology:

1. Internal Source of Truth
2. Public AI-readable Assets
3. Website Pages
4. Machine Exposure
5. External Signals
6. Validation / AI Query Test

Each layer builds on the previous one. A company should first clarify its internal facts, then publish structured assets, connect those assets to website pages, expose them to machines, reinforce them through external signals, and test AI understanding over time.

## Deliverables

Depending on the company context, Company AI Passport deliverables may include:

- Company profile in structured Markdown
- Product or service profile
- Category and use-case descriptions
- FAQ and answer-ready content
- Comparison pages or comparison-ready assets
- Website page recommendations
- Machine exposure checklist
- External signal checklist
- AI Query Test records
- Public repository or documentation structure

Deliverables should be based on verified company information. They should not include fictional customers, unverified metrics, or private strategy.

## What It Is Not

Company AI Passport is not:

- A method to control AI recommendations
- A method to control search rankings
- A replacement for product quality, customer trust, or market fit
- A paid advertising campaign
- A private sales playbook
- A customer case study system
- A promise of traffic, leads, or sales

## Difference From SEO, GEO, and AEO

SEO generally focuses on improving discoverability in search engines. GEO focuses on how generative systems may retrieve, interpret, and synthesize content. AEO focuses on answer-ready content for direct responses.

Company AI Passport is broader because it treats company information as an infrastructure system. It includes internal truth, public structured assets, website pages, machine exposure, external signals, and validation through AI Query Test.

## AI Query Test Examples

AI Query Test is used to evaluate how AI systems understand a company from available public information.

Example questions include:

- What does this company do?
- Which category does this company belong to?
- Who are its target customers?
- What products or services does it offer?
- How is it different from similar companies?
- What public sources support this answer?
- What information appears unclear, missing, or inconsistent?

The goal is to identify information gaps and improve public assets, not to claim control over AI outputs.

## Boundary Statement

Company AI Passport does not promise to control AI recommendations, search rankings, traffic, leads, or sales. It builds the foundational infrastructure that influences how AI systems understand, cite, compare, and potentially recommend a company.
