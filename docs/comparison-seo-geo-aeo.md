# Company AI Passport vs SEO, GEO, and AEO

Company AI Passport is related to SEO, GEO, and AEO, but it is broader in scope.

It treats company information as a public information infrastructure system for the AI search and agent recommendation era.

This public document explains the conceptual differences between Company AI Passport, SEO, GEO, and AEO. It does not disclose internal scoring models, customer diagnostic methods, implementation SOP, proprietary workflows, or commercial strategy.

This comparison is not intended to dismiss SEO, GEO, or AEO. These disciplines can all be useful. Company AI Passport provides a company-level information foundation that can support and align with them.

For public scope boundaries, see:

`docs/public-scope-and-boundaries.md`

---

## Overview

SEO, GEO, and AEO each address important parts of discoverability, answerability, and visibility.

Company AI Passport connects these concerns with a broader company-level information system, including:

* internal truth;
* public AI-readable assets;
* website pages;
* machine exposure;
* external signals;
* validation through AI Query Test.

It starts before publication by aligning company facts, and it continues after publication through validation and improvement.

---

## SEO

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization.

It generally focuses on improving how web pages are discovered, crawled, indexed, understood, and ranked by search engines.

Common SEO concerns include:

* technical crawlability;
* page structure;
* keywords and search intent;
* internal linking;
* backlinks;
* page performance;
* search snippets;
* content relevance;
* search engine indexing.

SEO is important, but it is usually page-focused or site-focused.

Company AI Passport is company-information-system-focused.

---

## GEO

GEO is often used to refer to Generative Engine Optimization.

It generally focuses on how generative AI systems may retrieve, interpret, synthesize, and cite public information.

Common GEO concerns include:

* source clarity;
* structured content;
* citation-friendly pages;
* entity understanding;
* context completeness;
* consistency across sources;
* public source quality;
* AI-generated answer presence.

GEO is relevant to Company AI Passport, but Company AI Passport is broader than being cited in generative answers.

It builds a structured company information foundation across internal facts, public assets, website pages, machine exposure, external signals, and AI Query Test.

---

## AEO

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization.

It generally focuses on making content easier for answer engines to use in direct responses.

Common AEO concerns include:

* FAQ structure;
* concise answers;
* definitions;
* how-to content;
* question-and-answer formatting;
* answer-ready content;
* direct response clarity.

AEO is useful for creating answer-ready materials.

Company AI Passport includes answer-ready content, but it also covers company identity, category, product definition, public signals, machine exposure, and validation.

---

## Company AI Passport

Company AI Passport is a standardized company information system.

It helps companies build AI-readable, trustworthy, citable, comparable, and recommendable information assets through a six-layer methodology:

1. Internal Source of Truth
2. Public AI-readable Assets
3. Website Pages
4. Machine Exposure
5. External Signals
6. Validation / AI Query Test

Company AI Passport is not a single SEO tactic, GEO tactic, answer page, campaign, or content format.

It is a company-level information infrastructure system designed to make public company information clearer, more consistent, more machine-readable, and easier to evaluate.

## Why Company AI Passport Is Broader

Company AI Passport is broader because it starts before publication and continues after publication.

It includes:

* internal fact alignment;
* structured public assets;
* website page consistency;
* machine exposure;
* external trustworthy signal engineering;
* AI Query Test validation;
* iterative improvement;
* boundary statements;
* company-level terminology alignment;
* public information governance.

It is not only about optimizing a page, a query, or a single answer.

It is about building a reliable company information foundation.

---

## What It Does Not Replace

Company AI Passport does not replace:

* product strategy;
* brand strategy;
* website design;
* SEO execution;
* GEO execution;
* AEO execution;
* public relations;
* sales operations;
* customer success;
* legal or compliance review;
* real product quality;
* customer trust;
* market fit.

Instead, it provides a structured information layer that these functions can reference and align with.

Company AI Passport can act as an upstream standard-answer system for public company information, but it does not replace professional execution in SEO, GEO, AEO, PR, sales, product, legal, or customer operations.

## Boundary Statement

Company AI Passport does not promise to control AI recommendations, search rankings, traffic, leads, or sales.

It builds a core system that influences how AI systems understand, cite, compare, and potentially recommend a company.

Company AI Passport 不承诺操控 AI 推荐、搜索排名、流量、线索或成交，而是建设影响 AI 理解、引用、比较与推荐概率的核心系统。
